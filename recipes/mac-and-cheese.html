Mac & Cheese Recipe

Description

This mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread
crumb topping is creamy and comforting. It's easy
to make the cheese sauce from scratch on your
stovetop, starting with a roux and adding milk,
Cheddar, and Parmesan, resulting in a rich,
decadent sauce that coats every nook and cranny
of the noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of uncooked Macaroni Noodles
  • Butter & Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt, Pepper and Paprika
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles: Boil the macaroni in salted
    water until the noodles are al dente. Drain and transfer to a
    prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the Cheese sauce: Melt butter, then whisk
    in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer, and stir in
    the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering
    until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  3. Make the topping:Melt two tablespoons of butter
    in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and toast until the crumbs
    are brown. Spread the topping over the macaroni and cheese,
    then sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheeseBake in the preheated oven
    until the topping is golden brown
